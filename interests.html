My Interests

Technical

As a full stack developer, I’m deeply intrigued by emerging technologies and how they are shaping the future of software engineering. Here are some of the areas I’m particularly excited about:

  • Web Frameworks & Tools: Angular 19 has been a key focus of mine recently, enabling the creation of highly interactive, efficient, and maintainable web applications. I’m always eager to explore new tools that improve developer productivity.
  • AI & Machine Learning Integration: The convergence of AI with software engineering is an ever growing topic. From incorporating chatbots into applications to leveraging machine learning for predictive analytics, there is no doubt AI is here to stay so it'll be interesting to how to tool gets applied.
  • Robotics: With a background in engineering, I’ve always been curious by the intersection of mechanical and software found with robots. From designing control systems to integrating sensors and AI, I enjoy keeping up to date with the latest developments in the feild.
  • Cybersecurity: While I don’t have prior experience in this field, I’m eager to dive into the world of cybersecurity. I’m particularly interested in learning how to identify vulnerabilities, safeguard systems, and stay ahead of emerging threats in an increasingly connected world.
